ABOUT THE ARTIST

STATEMENT

"I am not seeking to replicate nature, only translate its language into tangible textures."

I'm a self-taught artist working with organic abstraction and ethereal landscapes. I let nature's unpredictable fluidity guide me (the movement of water, the softness of light) while keeping things minimal so emotion and memory can come through without distraction.

I choose pigments that know how to surprise me. There's a conversation between what I intend and what the water decides, resulting in subtle layers, soft transitions, and colors that shift in unrepeatable ways. The final pieces aren't landscapes of places, but landscapes of feeling. Spaces meant to be felt as much as seen.
